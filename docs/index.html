Who is Bodhi?


Bodhi is a 2 year old pure bred european basset hound. He is about 10 inches off the ground. Bodhis ears are 12 inches each (30 cm). Bodhi is black and white in color with a little bit of brown. Bodhi was born at Stonewall Farm.

What is a Basset?

"Bassets are large, short, solid and long, with curved sabre tails held high over their long backs..." - Wikipedia.
